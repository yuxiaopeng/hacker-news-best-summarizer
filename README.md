## 2. 线上儿童安全年龄验证工具正在监控成年人

**原文标题**: Online age-verification tools for child safety are surveilling adults

**原文链接**: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/08/social-media-child-safety-internet-ai-surveillance.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/08/social-media-child-safety-internet-ai-surveillance.html)

美国各州旨在保障线上儿童安全的新法律要求进行年龄验证，却无意中将数百万成年人也纳入了批评者认为会对其进行监视的系统。近一半的美国州正在制定法律，要求平台（包括社交媒体、游戏和成人内容网站）验证用户年龄以阻止未成年人访问。

这些系统通常采用人工智能面部识别、年龄估算或政府身份证扫描，并常由第三方身份验证供应商管理。尽管一些平台旨在最小化数据收集，但全面的身份验证可能导致敏感数据（姓名、面部信息、地址）被保留多年。隐私倡导者警告称，集中存储如此大量的不可更改身份数据，会为黑客（如Discord泄露事件）和政府要求提供绝佳目标，对自由开放的互联网构成严重威胁。

像Discord这样的公司因强制提交身份信息而面临用户强烈反对，导致实施延迟。平衡儿童安全与用户隐私并最大程度地减少“摩擦”仍然是一项重大挑战，同时还存在对规避措施和盗版的担忧。

监管机构认为这些法律保护未成年人，并要求严格的数据最小化和安全性。然而，弗吉尼亚州的年龄验证法最近被联邦法院以违反第一修正案为由叫停。公民自由团体主张制定全面的联邦隐私法作为更有效的解决方案。

尽管面临法律斗争，年龄验证预计将成为线上服务的永久性组成部分。这一趋势指向“持续验证”，即创建并在一系列平台上重复使用单一的数字年龄证明，将身份验证从偶尔的障碍转变为成年人上网的内置日常层。

作者批评当前社交媒体上那些制造对人工智能恐惧和焦虑的言论，驳斥了不立即采用新工具就会变得毫无价值或落后于时代的说法，称其为“一派胡言”。他阐明人工智能并非是带来魔幻般改变的颠覆者，而仅仅是技术进步的延续，是一种用于搜索和优化的先进工具，而非科幻现象。

## 5. Yann LeCun raises $1B to build AI that understands the physical world

**原文标题**: Yann LeCun raises $1B to build AI that understands the physical world

**原文链接**: [https://www.wired.com/story/yann-lecun-raises-dollar1-billion-to-build-ai-that-understands-the-physical-world/](https://www.wired.com/story/yann-lecun-raises-dollar1-billion-to-build-ai-that-understands-the-physical-world/)

Yann LeCun, Meta's former chief AI scientist, has co-founded Advanced Machine Intelligence (AMI), a new Paris-based startup, raising over $1 billion at a $3.5 billion valuation. AMI aims to build "AI world models" that understand the physical world, possess persistent memory, reason, and plan.

LeCun argues this approach is essential for true human-level intelligence, dismissing the idea that scaling Large Language Models (LLMs) can achieve it, stating human reasoning is primarily physically grounded, not language-based. This positions AMI as a direct competitor to LLM-focused giants like OpenAI and Anthropic.

Key investors include Cathay Innovation, Greycroft, and Bezos Expeditions, alongside notable figures like Mark Cuban and Eric Schmidt. LeCun departed Meta because his world model research no longer aligned with Meta's shift to LLMs, believing enterprise applications are better pursued independently. AMI, co-founded with several Meta veterans, will target industries such as manufacturing, biomedical, and robotics, initially collaborating with partners like Toyota and Samsung.

LeCun advocates for open-source AI, believing its power necessitates democratic control rather than being dictated by individual companies or leaders. He plans for AMI to quickly release its first models, ultimately aiming for a "universal world model" to form the basis of generally intelligent systems across various industries.

## 9. Cloudflare crawl endpoint

**原文标题**: Cloudflare crawl endpoint

**原文链接**: [https://developers.cloudflare.com/changelog/post/2026-03-10-br-crawl-endpoint/](https://developers.cloudflare.com/changelog/post/2026-03-10-br-crawl-endpoint/)

Cloudflare has launched a new `/crawl` endpoint for its Browser Rendering service, now in open beta. This API allows users to crawl entire websites with a single call by submitting a starting URL. It automatically discovers pages via sitemaps and links, renders them in a headless browser, and returns content in HTML, Markdown, and structured JSON formats (powered by Workers AI).

Crawl jobs run asynchronously; users submit a URL, receive a job ID, and retrieve results later. Key features include extensive crawl scope controls (depth, page limits, URL patterns), automatic page discovery, and incremental crawling (using `modifiedSince` and `maxAge`). A static mode (`render: false`) is available for faster static site crawling.

The endpoint functions as a "well-behaved bot," respecting `robots.txt` directives and AI Crawl Control by default, making it suitable for training AI models, building RAG pipelines, or for research and content monitoring. It cannot bypass Cloudflare bot detection or captchas and self-identifies as a bot. The service is available on both Workers Free and Paid plans.
