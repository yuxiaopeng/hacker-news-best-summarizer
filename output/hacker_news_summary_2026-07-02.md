## 5. Most arguments are about ego, not ideas

**原文标题**: Most arguments are about ego, not ideas

**原文链接**: [https://wangcong.org/2026-06-30-why-i-stopped-arguing-with-people.html](https://wangcong.org/2026-06-30-why-i-stopped-arguing-with-people.html)

The author, a former software engineer, recounts his journey from zealously arguing for correctness to understanding the futility of it. He realized that being "correct" isn't inherently "good," as winning an argument creates a loser and often strengthens an opponent's resolve. Most arguments, he contends, are ego-driven, where challenging an idea is perceived as a personal attack, leading to defensive resistance rather than rational discourse.

Humans are primarily emotional, rationalizing feelings rather than reasoning to conclusions, rendering logic ineffective against ingrained beliefs. Correcting others, even with good intentions, is rarely appreciated or effective; people learn best from consequences. The only exception is when individuals explicitly ask for help, signaling an openness to advice.

Instead of debating, the author suggests leveraging differences as an asset, particularly in innovation and business, where unique perspectives create value. Fundamentally, one can only change oneself. Directing energy towards personal growth, through humble feedback-seeking, is far more productive than attempting to alter others. Letting go of the ego's need to win arguments fosters continuous improvement and a more peaceful experience of the world.

文章总结道，“所有权”的概念正日益被收回到由少数公司控制的账户和服务器中，稀释了数字时代“购买”的传统含义。

## 7. 朝日 Linux 7.1 进度报告

**原文标题**: Asahi Linux 7.1 Progress Report

**原文链接**: [https://asahilinux.org/2026/06/progress-report-7-1/](https://asahilinux.org/2026/06/progress-report-7-1/)

Asahi Linux 7.1 进展报告强调了关键进展和近期挑战的解决方案。macOS 27 出现了一个关键的启动问题，苹果的启动工具现在需要特定的 APFS 元数据标志，导致 Asahi 安装从启动选择器中消失。chaos_princess 解决了这个问题，新的 Asahi 安装会自动设置该标志，现有安装则可通过安装程序或新的 Linux 工具修复。

另一个 macOS 27 更新改变了 SMC 电池管理接口，导致 Linux 中出现紧急关机；这个问题已在内核 7.0.12+ 中得到修补。报告建议不要在生产机器上安装开发者测试版。

M3 系列机器取得了显著进展，包括高质量音频输出、CPU 频率切换、big.LITTLE 任务调度以及 SMC 硬件传感器支持，这主要得益于稳定的苹果硬件设计以及 chaos_princess 和 Yureka 的努力。PCIe、WiFi、蓝牙和 NVMe 的核心驱动程序也已可用，M3 的安装程序支持正在快速推进。

一项值得关注的进展是为苹果视频解码器（AVD）创建了自定义固件。为了规避苹果复杂且特定于 SoC 的固件，sofus 为 AVD 的 Cortex-M3 开发了一个基本的无状态固件，使 V4L2 驱动程序能够直接对 AVC 硬件进行编程，支持 10 位 4K 视频。未来计划包括支持 VP9、HEVC 和 AV1。

最后，m1n1 v1.6.0 发布，现在二级构建需要 Rust。该版本在 Rust 中集成了 GPU 初始化和苹果设备树解析，从而简化了内核驱动程序，并增加了 M3 改进，同时为 M4 和 A18 Pro 支持奠定了基础。

## 8. 戈多将不再接受 AI 编写的代码贡献

**原文标题**: Godot will no longer accept AI-authored code contributions

**原文链接**: [https://www.pcgamer.com/gaming-industry/open-source-game-engine-godot-will-no-longer-accept-ai-authored-code-contributions-we-cant-trust-heavy-users-of-ai-to-understand-their-code-enough-to-fix-it/](https://www.pcgamer.com/gaming-industry/open-source-game-engine-godot-will-no-longer-accept-ai-authored-code-contributions-we-cant-trust-heavy-users-of-ai-to-understand-their-code-enough-to-fix-it/)

Godot基金会正在禁止AI编写的代码贡献、AI代理提交的拉取请求以及人与人交流中AI生成的文本，理由是存在无法持续的“低质问题”。该开源游戏引擎的维护者在审查日益增多的AI生成贡献时，工作变得“越来越耗费精力且令人士气低落”。

这一决定旨在减轻维护者的负担，并保护新贡献者的培养机制。基金会强调AI不能为代码负责，贡献者必须对其提交的内容负起理解和修复的责任。审查AI生成的代码不提供任何指导机会，对志愿者来说越来越难以证明其合理性。

尽管披露后AI辅助“琐碎事务”是可以接受的，但完全禁止AI编写的代码和交流中AI生成的文本（不包括人类编写文本的机器翻译）是一项“基本的尊重原则”。Godot将保持保守态度，随着AI工具的发展重新评估相关政策。
