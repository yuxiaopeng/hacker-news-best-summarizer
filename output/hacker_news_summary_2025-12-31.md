**原文链接**: [https://screenrant.com/stranger-things-creator-turn-off-settings-premiere/](https://screenrant.com/stranger-things-creator-turn-off-settings-premiere/)

"Stranger Things" creator Ross Duffer has issued a public service announcement, urging viewers to adjust their TV settings before watching the season 5 premiere. Duffer, in an Instagram guide, advised fans to turn off several features he labeled "garbage," including dynamic contrast, super resolution, edge enhancer, color filter, and noise reduction.

He specifically singled out truemotion and smoothmotion as the biggest "offenders" that cause the "dreaded soap opera effect." Duffer also strongly warned against using "vivid" mode, stating it "destroys the color" and fundamentally deviates from the filmmaker's artistic intent. While advanced viewing presets like Dolby Vision Movie Dark can help, he stressed that viewers should manually confirm these altering settings are disabled.

Duffer's advice highlights a conflict between technological advancements designed to appeal to casual viewers with sharper or more colorful images, and creators' goals to preserve their original vision. By turning off these features, viewers can ensure they experience "Stranger Things" exactly as the creators intended.

"Stranger Things" season 5, volume 1, is currently streaming on Netflix. Volume 2 is set to release on December 25, 2025, with the series finale on December 31, 2025.

## 10. 软件开发的未来在于软件开发者

**原文标题**: The future of software development is software developers

**原文链接**: [https://codemanship.wordpress.com/2025/11/25/the-future-of-software-development-is-software-developers/](https://codemanship.wordpress.com/2025/11/25/the-future-of-software-development-is-software-developers/)

拥有43年经验的资深程序员兼本文作者驳斥了大型语言模型（LLMs）将取代软件开发人员的观点，认为这只是漫长且失败的同类预测循环中的最新一环。他回顾了从所见即所得编辑器、第四代编程语言（4GLs）到无代码平台等之前的技术，它们曾被同样吹捧为“程序员的终结”，但结果却始终是带来了更多的程序和更多的开发人员，这恰好印证了杰文斯悖论。

尽管作者承认当前“AI”炒作的规模前所未有，但他认为，与之前真正提高生产力的工具不同，LLMs往往阻碍开发，使软件变得更不可靠、更难以维护。他解释说，编程的根本挑战不在于语法，而在于将模糊的人类思维转化为精确、无歧义的计算逻辑——这项任务需要通用智能，而LLMs凭借其句法模型根本不具备这种能力。他强调，LLM生成的代码前后不一致，容易出错，需要人类专业知识来识别和纠正。

他对昂贵的超大规模LLMs的长期可行性持怀疑态度，将它们比作不可持续的“阿波罗登月任务”。作者预见未来，适度的“AI”助手或许能协助原型开发或次要任务，但人类软件开发人员仍将不可或缺地掌控全局，甚至数量可能还会增加。他建议雇主现在就招聘开发人员，预测一旦当前AI取代程序员的“狂热梦想”消散，对开发人员的需求将重新激增。
