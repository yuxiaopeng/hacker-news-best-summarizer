## 2. USCIS Will Grant 'Adjustment of Status' Only in Extraordinary Circumstances

**原文标题**: USCIS Will Grant 'Adjustment of Status' Only in Extraordinary Circumstances

**原文链接**: [https://www.uscis.gov/newsroom/news-releases/us-citizenship-and-immigration-services-will-grant-adjustment-of-status-only-in-extraordinary](https://www.uscis.gov/newsroom/news-releases/us-citizenship-and-immigration-services-will-grant-adjustment-of-status-only-in-extraordinary)

On May 22, 2026, U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) announced a new policy stating that "Adjustment of Status" – the process for non-citizens to apply for a Green Card while in the U.S. – will now only be granted in "extraordinary circumstances."

The new directive reiterates that, consistent with long-standing law, aliens seeking a Green Card must typically do so through consular processing at U.S. embassies or consulates abroad via the Department of State. USCIS officers are instructed to evaluate each case individually to determine if it warrants this "extraordinary form of relief."

USCIS Spokesman Zach Kahler explained that this policy aims to return to the "original intent of the law," ensuring individuals navigate the immigration system properly and preventing loopholes. He stated that temporary visitors, such as students, temporary workers, or tourists, are expected to leave when their visit ends, not use it as a first step to permanent residency.

The agency believes this approach will make the immigration system function as intended, reduce the need to track down those who overstay, and free up limited USCIS resources. These resources can then be reallocated to other priorities, including processing visas for victims of violent crime and human trafficking, and naturalization applications, ultimately making the system fairer and more efficient.

## 5. HN 发布：Superset (YC P26)——智能体时代的 IDE

**原文标题**: Launch HN: Superset (YC P26) – IDE for the agents era

**原文链接**: [https://github.com/superset-sh/superset](https://github.com/superset-sh/superset)

Superset (YC P26) 被介绍为“智能体时代”的IDE，一个专用于AI智能体的代码编辑器。它允许开发者同时并行地编排多个基于CLI的AI编程智能体，例如Claude Code、OpenAI Codex CLI和GitHub Copilot。

该平台的核心优势在于通过将每个任务隔离在独立的git工作区（git worktree）中，消除了上下文切换开销，确保智能体之间互不干扰。主要功能包括并行执行（10+智能体同时运行）、带通知的智能体监控、用于快速审查更改的内置diff查看器和编辑器，以及一键移交到外部IDE或终端。Superset与任何在终端中运行的CLI智能体都具有通用兼容性，并提供工作区预设以自动化环境设置。

目前适用于macOS，需要Bun v1.0+、Git 2.20+、GitHub CLI和Caddy。用户可以下载预构建版本或从源代码构建，并可自定义键盘快捷键和工作区配置。该项目在Elastic License 2.0下以源代码可用形式发布，强调隐私和对智能体连接的明确控制。Superset通过Discord、Twitter和GitHub鼓励社区参与，以获取支持、反馈和贡献。

Bolt 首席执行官 Ryan Breslow 透露他解散了公司的 HR 团队，声称他们“制造了不存在的问题”，这些问题在他们离开后就消失了。这项激进举措是他“战时”战略的一部分，旨在重振他联合创办的金融科技公司 Bolt。该公司在过度开支和糟糕的决策后，其估值从2022年的110亿美元暴跌至2024年预计的3亿美元。

他用一个规模更小、专注于基本培训和资源的“人才运营”团队取代了HR团队，以此将其与他所称的传统HR的“错误能量”区分开来。布雷斯洛还放弃了此前的一些福利，例如四天工作制和无限带薪休假，以使公司回归“艰苦奋斗”的状态。

尽管有布雷斯洛否认的拖欠工资传闻，他声称这一战略正在奏效。Bolt 已精简至约100名初级、更努力工作的员工，据报道，客户也得到了更好的服务。
