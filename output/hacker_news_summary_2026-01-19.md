## 2. jQuery 4

**原文标题**: jQuery 4

**原文链接**: [https://blog.jquery.com/2026/01/17/jquery-4-0-0/](https://blog.jquery.com/2026/01/17/jquery-4-0-0/)

jQuery 4.0.0 于 2026 年 1 月 17 日发布，标志着这个 JavaScript 库的 20 周年，也是其近十年来首次主要版本更新。此次发布带来了显著的现代化和改进，同时伴随着一些破坏性变更。提供了升级指南和 jQuery Migrate 插件以帮助过渡。

主要变更包括移除对 IE 10 及更早版本（IE 11 支持将在 jQuery 5.0 中移除）、Edge Legacy 以及非常旧的 iOS、Firefox 和 Android Browser 版本的支持。jQuery 4.0 新增了对 Trusted Types 和内容安全策略 (CSP) 的支持，现在大多数异步脚本请求都使用 `<script>` 标签以避免 CSP 错误。

jQuery 源代码已从 AMD 迁移到 ES 模块，增强了与现代构建工具的兼容性。许多长期弃用的 API，如 `jQuery.isArray`、`jQuery.parseJSON` 和 `jQuery.trim` 已被移除，推荐使用原生 JavaScript 的等效方法。内部专用的 `push`、`sort` 和 `splice` 方法也已从 jQuery 原型中移除。

一个行为上的破坏性变更是焦点事件的顺序，现在它遵循 W3C 规范，与 jQuery 之前保持一致的顺序有所不同。"slim" 构建变得更小，移除了 Deferreds 和 Callbacks，转而支持原生 Promises（IE11 需要主构建版本或 polyfill）。总的来说，该版本实现了超过 3k 字节（gzip 压缩后）的体积减小。

jQuery 4.0.0 可通过 jQuery CDN 和 npm 获取。

## 4. American importers and consumers bear the cost of 2025 tariffs: analysis

**原文标题**: American importers and consumers bear the cost of 2025 tariffs: analysis

**原文链接**: [https://www.kielinstitut.de/publications/americas-own-goal-who-pays-the-tariffs-19398/](https://www.kielinstitut.de/publications/americas-own-goal-who-pays-the-tariffs-19398/)

The Kiel Policy Brief, "America’s Own Goal: Who Pays the Tariffs?", concludes that American importers and consumers bear almost the entire cost of the 2025 US tariffs. Authors Hinz, Lohmann, Mahlkow, and Vorwig found that a striking 96% of the tariff burden is passed through to US buyers, with foreign exporters absorbing only about 4%.

This analysis utilizes shipment-level data covering over 25 million transactions valued at nearly $4 trillion, revealing near-complete pass-through of tariffs to US import prices. Consequently, US customs revenue is projected to surge by approximately $200 billion in 2025, a tax predominantly paid by Americans.

Event studies examining discrete tariff shocks on Brazil (50%) and India (25–50%) further confirm these findings: export prices did not decline. Instead, trade volumes collapsed. Indian export customs data validated this, showing that Indian exporters maintained their prices and reduced shipments when facing US tariffs, rather than absorbing the cost. The study ultimately characterizes these tariffs as an "own goal" for the US, with Americans bearing the financial brunt.

**原文标题**: Dead Internet Theory

**原文链接**: [https://kudmitry.com/articles/dead-internet-theory/](https://kudmitry.com/articles/dead-internet-theory/)

**原文标题**: Predicting OpenAI's ad strategy

**原文链接**: [https://ossa-ma.github.io/blog/openads](https://ossa-ma.github.io/blog/openads)

作者强烈反驳近期预测OpenAI将消亡的分析，坚称该公司财务状况稳健，估值达2600亿美元，并已获得400亿美元融资。尽管烧钱速度惊人（2025年预计80-120亿美元），OpenAI仍预计2025年年度经常性收入（ARR）将达到200亿美元，并准备进行首次公开募股（IPO）。

文章预测OpenAI的广告策略将成为主要的收入驱动力，将其定位为类似于谷歌搜索的“高意图”平台，而非社交媒体。已确认的广告产品包括答案底部、搜索结果中和侧边栏的赞助内容，将于2026年前在美国向免费版和Go版用户推出。广告将“明确标注”，独立于答案内容，尊重用户隐私，并提供用户控制选项。付费版本（Plus、Pro、Business、Enterprise）将保持无广告。

OpenAI的策略还包括自助广告平台、联盟佣金和对话式广告。随着Fidji Simo（前Meta广告变现负责人）担任应用程序CEO，该公司预计将建立完整的广告技术栈。

作者预测了一个“超级看涨的平均用户收入（ARPU）轨迹”：2026年为5.50美元，到2029年增至50美元，并与Perplexity的高CPM（每千次展示费用）进行了比较。考虑到保守的用户增长和当前收入，总收入预测如下：
*   **2026年：** 300-350亿美元（其中广告收入约52亿美元）
*   **2029年：** 1400-1500亿美元（其中广告收入约700亿美元）

文章最后略带讽刺地总结道：“世界即广告”，将广告定性为实现AGI（通用人工智能）并普及AI的“必要之恶”。

## 9. 基于蓝牙运行的去中心化点对点消息应用

**原文标题**: A decentralized peer-to-peer messaging application that operates over Bluetooth

**原文链接**: [https://bitchat.free/](https://bitchat.free/)

bitchat is a decentralized peer-to-peer messaging application that operates over Bluetooth mesh networks, requiring no internet, servers, or phone numbers. Unlike traditional apps that depend on centralized infrastructure, bitchat creates ad-hoc communication networks using only devices in physical proximity.

Each device acts as both a client and server, automatically discovering peers and relaying messages across multiple hops to extend the network's reach. This architecture provides censorship resistance, surveillance resistance, and infrastructure independence, ensuring communication remains functional during internet outages, natural disasters, protests, or in areas with limited connectivity.

The software is available for iOS/macOS (16.0+/13.0+) via the App Store and GitHub, and for Android (8.0+) on the Play Store and GitHub releases, with full protocol compatibility between versions. A technical whitepaper offers further documentation, and the software is released into the public domain by permissionlesstech.
